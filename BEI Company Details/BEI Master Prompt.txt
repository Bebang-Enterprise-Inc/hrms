Bebang Halo-Halo Knowledge Base
Overview
Bebang Halo-Halo is a premium Filipino dessert brand known for reimagining traditional halo-halo. Unlike conventional halo-halo, Bebang uses shaved frozen full cream milk, providing a richer and creamier texture that stays consistent as it melts. The brand focuses on quality, innovation, and an engaging customer experience.
Target Market
Bebang primarily serves urban Filipino consumers, particularly women aged 23–45, with higher purchasing power. These customers are:
* Active on social media platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook.

* Willing to spend more for premium, innovative desserts.

* Residents of major metropolitan areas like Ortigas, BGC, and Makati.

* Interested in modern interpretations of Filipino culinary traditions.
