Ronald Ron Dimatatac is currently working as Financial Controller for MacroAsia Corporation. He is a former 
Senior Associate in the Assurance practice of SGV & Co. / Ernst & Young Philippines. He joined the firm in 
September 2014 after passing the CPA board exam in July 2014. He graduated with a degree in Bachelor of Science 
in Accountancy at the Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Muntinlupa. He is a fourth year student at Centro Escolar 
University School of Law and Jurisprudence pursuing a Juris Doctor degree. 
 
He has over four (4) years of in-depth experience in the audit of shipping & chartering company, mining, hedge 
funds, investment holding company, hospitality, condominium corporation, leasing and restaurant company. He has 
handled offshore audit of hedge funds and shipping & chartering companies after transferring from local audit to 
Asia-Pacific Talent Hub in December 2015. While being part of the Asia-Pacific Talent Hub, he has already 
experienced handling clients based in several countries such as Hong Kong, Australia, Switzerland and Cayman 
Island. He has been seconded to Hong Kong for four months for audit works. 
 
Progressed from Financial Accountant to Financial Reporting Manager, and currently Financial Controller,
